Wake Up and Smell the Coffee: A Better Investment than Stocks?

21 Mar, 2023

Are you tired of hearing about the stock market and its unpredictable nature? Do you want to invest your money in something that is guaranteed to bring you joy every morning? Look no further than your local coffee shop. Yes, you heard that right. The coffee habit may just be a better investment than the stock market.

Why Waste Time on WallStreetBets When You Can Invest in Your Caffeine Fix?

Firstly, let’s talk about the stock market. It’s a rollercoaster ride that can leave you feeling nauseous and broke. One day you’re up, the next day you’re down. It’s like playing a game of roulette, but with your hard-earned money. On the other hand, investing in your daily caffeine fix is a sure thing. You know exactly what you’re getting, and it’s always a delicious, warm cup of happiness.

The coffee habit has a much higher return on investment than the stock market. Sure, stocks may give you a return of 6-8%, but coffee gives you a return of 100% every morning. That’s right, you invest a few dollars every day, and you get a cup of coffee that makes you feel like you can conquer the world. Can the stock market do that? I don’t think so.

You don’t have to worry about market crashes or economic recessions. Coffee shops will always be there, serving up your favorite drinks. Plus, you can always switch up your order and try something new. Good luck doing that with your stocks.

So, next time someone asks you about your investment portfolio, proudly tell them about your daily caffeine fix. After all, it’s the little things in life that bring us the most joy.
