Why n8n's $7.2M Revenue Growth Should Make Every Business Leader Rethink Workflow Automation

23 Jun, 2025

Are you still manually juggling repetitive tasks while your competitors automate their way to efficiency? The explosive growth of n8n, a workflow automation platform that hit $7.2 million in revenue in 2024 with just 59 employees, signals a fundamental shift in how businesses approach operational efficiency.

For those new to the concept, workflow automation is the process of streamlining and automating repetitive business tasks using software, allowing teams to focus on more strategic work.

The Numbers Don’t Lie: Automation Is No Longer Optional

With over 230,000 active users and recognition as Germany’s fifth fastest-growing startup in 2024, n8n represents more than just another software success story. It’s a wake-up call for organizations still relying on manual processes.

The platform’s 109.5k GitHub stars place it among the most popular open-source projects, while its 4.9/5 rating on G2 demonstrates real user satisfaction. But here’s what should concern you: if your business isn’t leveraging similar automation tools, you’re likely falling behind competitors who are.

Note: Open-source software means the code is freely available for anyone to view, modify, and distribute, often leading to more innovative and cost-effective solutions.

What Makes n8n Different—And Why It Matters to Your Bottom Line

Unlike traditional automation tools that lock you into proprietary ecosystems, n8n offers something revolutionary: complete control over your data and workflows. As highlighted by Code Boost, the platform combines visual workflow building with custom JavaScript capabilities, making it accessible to both technical and non-technical teams.

The self-hosting capability isn’t just a technical feature—it’s a strategic advantage. While competitors like Zapier process your sensitive data on their servers, n8n allows you to maintain complete privacy and control. For businesses handling confidential information or operating under strict compliance requirements, this distinction could be make-or-break.

The Hidden Cost of Inaction

Consider this: one Reddit user reported scaling from 1.5 million to 9.5 million operations monthly while reducing costs by 23.75x compared to alternatives. If your organization processes similar volumes manually or through expensive automation services, you’re potentially hemorrhaging resources.

The platform’s 700+ app integrations mean it can connect virtually any system in your tech stack. From CRM synchronization to AI-powered data processing, n8n eliminates the silos that plague modern businesses.

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management - software that helps businesses manage interactions with customers and potential customers.

Major Players Are Already Moving

Companies like UPS, Northstar Aerospace, and Bandwidth have already integrated n8n into their operations. These aren’t small startups experimenting with new tools—they’re established enterprises recognizing that workflow automation is now a competitive necessity, not a luxury.

The Strategic Question You Must Answer

As n8n continues its trajectory—powering automation for over 100,000 developers and 20,000 companies—the question isn’t whether workflow automation will transform your industry. It’s whether you’ll lead that transformation or be left scrambling to catch up.

The platform’s modular, node-based architecture and AI integration capabilities position it perfectly for the next wave of business automation. While your competitors automate routine tasks and focus human talent on strategic initiatives, can you afford to maintain the status quo?

The bottom line: n8n’s remarkable growth reflects a broader market reality. Businesses that embrace intelligent automation today will define tomorrow’s competitive landscape. Those that don’t may find themselves questioning not just their efficiency, but their relevance.
