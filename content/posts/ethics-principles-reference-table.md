This table outlines fundamental ethical principles applicable across various domains, including healthcare, law, and business. Each principle is defined, illustrated with a practical example, and justified by its importance in fostering ethical conduct. The principles encompass respect for autonomy, non-maleficence, beneficence, justice, veracity, fidelity, and self-respect, collectively forming a framework for moral decision-making and interpersonal interactions. This concise overview serves as a foundational guide to understanding and applying these core ethical values in professional and personal contexts.

## ENG

<table class="has-fixed-layout"><thead><tr><th>Principle</th><th>Definition</th><th>Example</th><th>Why it’s important</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Respect for Autonomy</td><td>Respect individuals’ freedoms and decision-making abilities</td><td>A patient has the right to refuse medical treatment.</td><td>Ensures individuals’ decisions are honored and respected.</td></tr><tr><td>Non-Maleficence</td><td>Avoid causing harm to others</td><td>A doctor should not prescribe medication that could cause harm.</td><td>Prevents harm to others, ensuring actions are not detrimental to well-being or safety.</td></tr><tr><td>Beneficence</td><td>Act in ways that benefit others</td><td>A nurse provides emotional support to a patient.</td><td>Promotes others’ health, happiness, and well-being.</td></tr><tr><td>Justice</td><td>Act fairly and impartially</td><td>A judge applies the law equally to all parties.</td><td>Ensures fairness, equity, and impartiality.</td></tr><tr><td>Veracity</td><td>Act with honesty and truthfulness</td><td>A company does not misrepresent its products.</td><td>Promotes trust and credibility.</td></tr><tr><td>Fidelity</td><td>Fulfill commitments and obligations</td><td>An employee honors their contract.</td><td>Demonstrates respect for commitments and reinforces bonds between individuals and organizations.</td></tr><tr><td>Self-Respect</td><td>Value and respect oneself and one’s moral values</td><td>A person prioritizes self-care.</td><td>Promotes a positive sense of identity, confidence, and personal growth.</td></tr></tbody></table>
